Harbor Community Clinic

Founded more than 40 years ago Harbor Community Clinic is a trusted provider of state-of-the-art health services for men, women and children throughout San Pedro, CA, and the surrounding Los Angeles and South Bay areas. he clinic is dedicated to providing low-cost and no-cost health services to residents with low incomes and those whose employers do not provide health insurance coverage.

Harbor Interfaith

The mission of Harbor Interfaith Services is to empower the homeless and working poor to achieve self-sufficiency by providing support services including shelter, transitional housing, food, job placement, advocacy, childcare, education, and life-skills training.

Toberman Neighborhood Center

Toberman Neighborhood Center is the premier social service agency in the Harbor area, serving families, children, youth, and adults. For over 100 years, Toberman Neighborhood purpose has been to support the most vulnerable. As a result, we continue to make true on our promise: to change lives, one at a time.

Los Angeles Suicide Prevention Center

The National Suicide Prevention Lifeline provides free and confidential emotional support to people in suicidal crisis or emotional distress 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, across the United States. The Lifeline is comprised of a national network of over 150 local crisis centers, combining custom local care and resources with national standards and best practices.
