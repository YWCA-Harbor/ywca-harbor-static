Donations We Accept and Sell

The store will accept the majority of donations delivered although the managers reserve the right to refuse donations due to space. Donations can be brought to the store directly Monday through Saturday between the hours of 9:00 a.m. and 5:00 p.m. We are closed on Sundays.
