Our Partners

We are deeply grateful to our partners, listed below, for their generous contributions of time, goods and funds. Together, we address complex issues and successfully leverage our resources to provide essential programming and services. With support from our partners, we can continue to fund existing programs, and create new services in response to emerging needs.

We exist to bring an end to modern slavery by rescuing victims, restoring lives, and reforming society.
